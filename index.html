Personal Budget Calculator

How to use Select your currency then fill in the form with your predicted expenditure for the month. Each box has the option to calculate daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly. Select the appropriate option for each item. If more boxes are needed you can create more by clicking the + button and remove them by clicking the x button. Once all of your details have been entered, click the calculate button to view results. Form data can be amended and re-calculated if you wish to see how the changes impact on the results.
